Panelization Methods

MethodDescriptionBest For
V-ScoringV-groove cuts on both sides, leaving a thin web for snap-out depanelization. 30/45/60 angles. Remaining thickness 0.3-0.5mm.Rectangular boards, high-volume, cost-effective
Tab RoutingRouted outline with breakaway tabs (mouse bites). 5-8 perforated tabs per board side. No stress on components.Irregular shapes, heavy components, medium volume
Full RoutingComplete CNC rout with zero-connection separation. Boards delivered fully singulated.Complex outlines, high-value boards, clean edges required
Mixed V-Score + RouteCombined approach — V-score on straight edges, route on curves. Optimizes cost and quality.Boards with mixed straight and curved edges
