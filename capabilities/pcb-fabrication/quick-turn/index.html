Design Qualification

What Qualifies for Quick-Turn

Complete Gerber data — all layers, drill file, board outline, and fabrication notes included at submission
Standard materials — FR-4 with Tg 130-180, standard copper weights 1-4oz inner, 1-6oz outer
2-16 layer count — higher layers and hybrid laminates move to standard production
No special processes — back-drill, edge plating, carbon ink, and countersinks not available on rush tiers
Panelized or single PCB — we handle panelization; provide individual board outline if you need routing
Clear fab notes — specify stackup, impedance requirements, and controlled impedance layer pairs upfront

DFM & Quality

Same-Day DFM, Zero Compromises

Speed doesn't mean cutting corners. Every quick-turn order goes through the same rigorous DFM review process — just faster. Our CAM engineers review your Gerber files within 4 hours of submission. If issues are found, you'll receive a marked-up report with specific recommendations before we proceed. Common catches include insufficient annular rings, acid traps, slivers, and solder mask sliver violations.
