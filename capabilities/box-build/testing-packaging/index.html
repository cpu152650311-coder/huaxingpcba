Proving Performance

System-Level Testing

01

Functional System Test

Custom-built FCT fixtures validate complete product functionality — power-on sequence, all I/O ports, user interface, communication protocols, sensor calibration, and load testing at rated specifications. Test scripts maintained per product revision with full pass/fail logging.

02

Burn-In & Aging

Products run continuously under load at elevated ambient temperature (40C–60C) for 4 to 48 hours, per your reliability specification. Catches infant-mortality failures before products leave the factory. Temperature and current draw logged throughout for trend analysis.

03

Vibration & Drop Testing

Random vibration per ISTA 3A and MIL-STD-810 profiles to simulate transit conditions. Guided drop tests at specified orientations. Verifies mechanical integrity of enclosure mounting, connector retention, and internal harness routing before packaging design sign-off.

Testing That Ships Confidence

Every product leaving our box-build line passes a mandatory final QC gate. For standard products, this includes a full functional test on 100% of units. For safety-critical or high-reliability applications, we implement sampling or 100% burn-in per your AQL and reliability requirements.

Our test engineers work from your test specification document, or we develop the test plan collaboratively based on your product requirements. Custom test fixtures — bed-of-nails, pogo-pin interfaces, pneumatic actuation, automated data acquisition — are designed and built in-house, typically within 5–8 working days of receiving your specification.

Documentation Package

Each production batch ships with a comprehensive quality documentation package: certificate of conformance (CoC), detailed test report with per-unit results, first-article inspection report (FAIR) per AS9102 format, X-ray images for BGA/QFN assemblies, and material certifications for enclosures, cables, and coatings.

Documentation is delivered as PDF in English (Chinese or bilingual available on request), organized per your document control requirements.
