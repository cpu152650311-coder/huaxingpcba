Wire & Cable Options

Wire Types We Work With

Most Common

UL-Approved PVC Wire

UL 1007/1015/1061 series. 300V or 600V rating, 80C to 105C temperature range. AWG 28 to AWG 10 available. Solid and stranded conductors. Ideal for internal appliance wiring, control panels, and consumer electronics. Full UL traceability with lot-level certification.

High Temperature

Silicone & PTFE Wire

Silicone rubber insulated (UL 3122/3135): 150C to 200C continuous. PTFE/Teflon (UL 1330/1332): 200C rating with exceptional chemical resistance. Used in automotive engine compartments, industrial ovens, medical sterilization equipment, and aerospace harnesses.

Specialized

Ribbon, Coaxial & Shielded

Flat ribbon cable (0.635mm/1.27mm pitch) with IDC connectors. Miniature coaxial (RG178/RG316) for RF interconnects. Braided-shield multi-conductor cable with drain wire termination. Twinax and differential-pair harnesses for high-speed data.

Connector Crimping & Termination

We terminate the full range of connector systems used in modern electronics: JST (PH/XH/PA/EH/VH series), Molex (Mini-Fit, Micro-Fit, PicoBlade, CLIK-Mate), TE/AMP (MATE-N-LOK, FASTON, Micro-MaTch), Hirose (DF13/DF52), JAE, and Samtec. Automated crimping machines with force monitoring ensure consistent gas-tight crimps on every terminal — no intermittent connections in the field.

For board-to-wire applications, we solder wires directly to PCB pads when connectors are impractical — strain-relieved with adhesive-lined heat-shrink and secured with cable ties to dedicated anchor points on the board or enclosure.

Seamless Integration with PCB Assembly

Your harnesses are built alongside your PCB assemblies — not at a separate supplier. This means the harness team has access to the actual PCBs, connectors, and enclosures during fabrication. Pre-fit checks on real hardware eliminate the back-and-forth of working with drawings alone.

Every harness is bundled, labeled with your part number and revision, and kitted with the corresponding PCBs and enclosures for final box-build assembly. Cable management inside the enclosure — routing clips, spiral wrap, braided sleeving, cable ties with mounting bases — is performed at the system integration station as part of the full build process.

Harness Capabilities

Wire Gauge Range

AWG 32 to AWG 4/0. Single-conductor, multi-conductor, shielded, twisted-pair, flat ribbon. Metric equivalents stocked for European customers.

Termination Types

Open-barrel crimp, closed-barrel crimp, insulation-displacement (IDC), solder cup, PCB solder (through-hole and SMD), screw terminal, spring clamp.

Strain Relief & Protection

Adhesive-lined dual-wall heat-shrink (3:1 and 4:1 ratio), molded strain relief boots, spiral wrap, braided expandable sleeving (PET/nomex), grommet edging.

Labeling & Identification

Heat-shrink tube printing (Brady/M21 series), self-laminating wrap-around labels, cable marker ties. Text, barcode, QR code. Resistant to IPA, MEK cleaning.

Certifications

IPC/WHMA-A-620 Class 2 and Class 3. UL ZPFW2/ZPFW8 recognized. ISO 9001. IATF 16949 for automotive harnesses. UL traceability per spool.

Production Volume

Single prototype harness to 10,000+ units/month. Semi-automated cut/strip/crimp machines for volume. Manual bench for complex or low-volume assemblies.
