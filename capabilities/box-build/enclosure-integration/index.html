Mechanical Fit & Sealing

Every enclosure goes through a first-article mechanical fit verification before production begins. We check PCB-to-enclosure alignment, connector cutout positioning, standoff height tolerances, and mounting hole registration against your 3D model or mechanical drawing.

For outdoor and industrial applications, we offer IP65/IP67-rated sealing with custom silicone or EPDM gaskets, O-ring compression seals, and cable gland integration. Every sealed unit undergoes a pressure-decay leak test as part of final QC, ensuring your product survives rain, dust, and washdown environments in the field.

Branding & Customization

Your product needs to look like yours. We apply custom branding during assembly: laser-etched logos and model numbers, silk-screen printed legends on front/rear panels, color-matched enclosure painting, and custom membrane keypad or overlay installation.

Serial number labeling with barcode or QR code is applied per your specification — thermal-transfer printed labels on polyester or polyimide substrate, rated for the operating environment. Regulatory marks (CE, FCC, UL, UKCA) are positioned per your compliance documentation on every unit.
