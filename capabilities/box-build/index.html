Beyond PCB Assembly

Complete Product Integration

Box-build goes beyond soldering components onto a PCB. We take your assembled boards and integrate them into enclosures — mounting, securing, wiring, labeling, and testing the complete product exactly as your end customer will receive it.

This eliminates the need to manage multiple suppliers for PCB assembly, enclosure fabrication, wiring harnesses, and final assembly. One partner manages the entire process — from bare PCB procurement to finished, tested, packaged product.

What We Integrate

01

Enclosure Assembly

Mounting PCBs into plastic, aluminum, or sheet metal enclosures. Standoffs, brackets, gaskets, EMI shielding, and IP-rated sealing. Custom enclosure sourcing available.

02

Wiring & Harnesses

Internal cabling, wire-to-board connectors, ribbon cables, and custom wiring harnesses. Crimped, soldered, and 100% continuity-tested. UL-certified wire and connectors.

03

Conformal Coating

Acrylic, silicone, and polyurethane coating via selective robotic spray or manual application. IPC-CC-830 verified thickness. UV inspection on every board.

04

Potting & Encapsulation

Epoxy and silicone potting for harsh-environment protection. Thermal management potting for power electronics. Vacuum degassing for bubble-free encapsulation.

05

Labeling & Branding

Serial number labels, regulatory markings (CE, FCC, UL), barcode/RFID tagging, and custom branding. Laser etching and silk-screen printing available for enclosures.

06

Final Packaging

Retail-ready or bulk packaging per your specification. Custom foam inserts, ESD-safe bags, user manual insertion, accessory kitting, and palletization for export.

Box-Build Capabilities

Enclosure Types

Plastic (ABS, PC, PA), aluminum extrusion, die-cast aluminum, sheet metal (steel, stainless), IP65/IP67 rated enclosures

Fastening Methods

Screw fastening (torque-controlled), press-fit, adhesive bonding, ultrasonic welding, thermal staking, rivet insertion

Connector Types

Circular (M5/M8/M12), D-Sub, USB, RJ45, terminal blocks, board-to-wire, FFC/FPC, RF coaxial

Testing Capability

HI-POT (dielectric withstand), ground bond, leakage current, functional system test with custom fixtures, burn-in / aging test

Certifications

IPC-A-610 Class 2 & 3, IPC/WHMA-A-620 (cable & harness), IPC-CC-830 (coating), ISO 9001, IATF 16949

Volume Range

Prototype (5–50 units) to mid-volume production (5,000–50,000 units/year). Flexible lines — quick changeover between products.

Supply Chain

Enclosure sourcing (CNC, injection molding, sheet metal), custom packaging design, accessory kitting, global logistics

Quality Assurance

In-process QC at each station, first-article inspection per product, 100% functional test, retained samples, traceability per serial number
