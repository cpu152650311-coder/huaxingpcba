Procurement Models

Choose the Model That Fits Your Supply Chain

Consigned / Kitted

You supply all components. We manage incoming inspection, inventory, and kitting.

  • You control the supply chain
  • 100% incoming visual & count verification
  • Moisture-sensitive (MSL) handling & baking
  • Secure dry storage, ESD inventory management
  • Kit-per-PCBA with BOM line-item traceability
  • Shortage & overage reporting per BOM line

Turn-Key

We procure everything. You receive assembled, tested PCBs — one invoice, one supplier.

  • Single point of accountability — we own the full supply chain
  • 200+ authorized distributor network across China/Asia
  • BOM cost optimization — alternative sourcing, volume aggregation
  • EOL/obsolescence proactive notification & last-time buy management
  • Open BOM pricing — you see every line-item cost
  • 12-month warranty on all procured components

Partial Turn-Key

You supply critical/specialty parts. We procure the rest — the best of both.

  • You consign ASICs, FPGAs, proprietary ICs, long-lead items
  • We source passives, connectors, standard ICs, discretes
  • Reduce your procurement workload by 70%
  • Maintain control over strategic components
  • Hybrid inventory management with clear ownership boundaries
  • No cross-contamination — consigned and procured lots are segregated

Counterfeit Prevention

Counterfeit semiconductors cost the industry an estimated $75B annually. Our multi-layer defense catches them before they reach your boards.

01

Authorized Supply Chain Only

We source exclusively through franchised/authorized distributors and direct manufacturer channels. No open-market or grey-market procurement. Full chain-of-custody documentation on request.

02

Visual & Dimensional Inspection

High-magnification microscope check on every incoming lot. Part marking consistency, lead frame integrity, package dimensions vs. datasheet. Blacktopping, remarking, and sanding detected.

03

X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF)

RoHS compliance verification and lead-frame material composition analysis. Non-compliant or substituted materials flagged instantly. Performed on every new supplier batch.

04

Decapsulation & Die Verification

For high-value / high-criticality ICs: acid decapsulation and die-level microscope comparison against OEM reference. Die markings, layout, and feature size verified. Optional, on request.

BOM Intelligence

Don't Let a Single Line Item
Stop Production

Nothing halts an assembly line faster than an unavailable component. Our BOM management team monitors every line item against EOL notices, allocation constraints, and lead-time shifts — so you're never surprised by a shortage.

  • EOL / NRND monitoring — proactive alerts with last-time-buy windows
  • Cross-reference engine — FFF (form-fit-function) alternatives with manufacturer validation
  • Multi-BOM aggregation — if you have multiple products sharing common parts, we optimize across all of them
  • Safety-stock management — we can hold buffer stock for long-lead or allocation-prone items
  • Alternative MPN recommendations — pin-compatible, drop-in replacements where the original is constrained
