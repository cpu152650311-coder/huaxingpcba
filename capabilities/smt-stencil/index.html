Why Our Stencils

Precision That Prints Perfectly

Laser-Cut Precision

Fiber laser cutting with ±5μm positional accuracy. Smooth aperture walls for consistent paste release. Every stencil 100% inspected under microscope before shipping.

24-Hour Turnaround

Standard stencils ship within 24 hours of Gerber approval. Same-day rush available for framed and frameless stencils. No minimum order — single stencil to volume orders.

Nano-Coating Technology

Proprietary nano-coating reduces solder paste bridging, improves release for fine-pitch apertures down to 0.3mm pitch QFNs and 0201 passives. Extends stencil life 3–5×.

Step Stencils

Multi-level thickness in a single stencil — thick areas for power components, thin for fine-pitch. Optimized paste volume for mixed-technology boards without compromising yield.

Premium Materials

304 stainless steel foil, 0.1–0.2mm thickness. Precision-tensioned mesh-mounted frames (736×736mm standard, custom sizes available). Aluminum frames with mesh border.

DFM Feedback Included

Every stencil order includes free solder paste DFM review. We flag aperture ratio issues, insufficient paste volume risks, and suggest optimizations — before we cut.

From Gerber to Finished Stencil

Upload your solder paste Gerber layer and we handle the rest. Our CAM engineers extract apertures, optimize for your component mix, and laser-cut to exact specifications. Every stencil is verified against your original Gerber data before packaging.

We stock standard framed sizes (584×584mm, 650×650mm, 736×736mm) and can manufacture custom sizes for unique SMT line configurations. Frameless (foil-only) stencils are also available for universal tensioning frames.
