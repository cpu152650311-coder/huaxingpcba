Incoterms: Your Choice

DDP — Delivered Duty Paid

We handle everything: freight, insurance, import duties, and taxes. You receive the package at your door — no customs paperwork, no surprise fees. Recommended for first-time buyers and prototypes.

DAP — Delivered at Place

We cover freight and insurance to your named destination. You handle import clearance and any local duties/taxes. Good balance for buyers with established customs brokers.

FOB — Free on Board

Goods delivered to Shenzhen port, cleared for export. You arrange and pay for ocean/air freight, insurance, and destination charges. Preferred by experienced importers with their own forwarders.

EXW — Ex Works

Goods available at our factory. You arrange all transportation, export clearance, freight, and import. Best when you have a consolidated shipment or existing logistics partner in China.
