ADAS Control Unit: 8-Layer HDI

A Tier-1 automotive supplier needed an ADAS camera control unit PCB with stringent thermal and reliability requirements. The design demanded 8-layer HDI with 0.3mm pitch BGA, controlled impedance on all high-speed lanes, and IPC Class 3 throughout — all at a production volume of 50,000 units/year.

Solution: We proposed a modified stackup using Panasonic Megtron 6 laminate for superior signal integrity, implemented laser-drilled microvias with stacked-via architecture, and built a custom flying-probe test program covering 100% of nets. First-pass yield stabilized at 99.2% within 3 production batches.

A medical device startup developing a wearable patient monitor needed a compact 6-layer rigid-flex PCB with ultra-fine 2.5/2.5mil traces, ENIG finish, and the ability to fold into a wrist-worn form factor. They were on a tight FDA submission timeline and needed functional prototypes in 10 days.
