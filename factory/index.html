Inside the Facility

PCB Fabrication Zone

Dedicated 6,000㎡ clean-room environment for bare PCB production. Automated etching, plating, drilling, and solder mask lines. Separate areas for prototype and volume production to avoid cross-contamination.

  • CNC Drilling (6-head)
  • LDI Exposure
  • VCP Plating Lines
  • Vacuum Etching
  • AOI Inspection
  • Flying Probe Test

SMT Assembly Zone

Eight fully automated high-speed SMT lines in a 4,000㎡ ESD-protected area. Each line: solder paste printer → 3D SPI → high-speed placers → 10-zone reflow → inline AOI. Capacity: 8M placements/day.

  • Yamaha YSM20R
  • Koh Young SPI
  • Omron AOI
  • Heller Reflow
  • Ersa Selective Wave
  • Nordson Coating

Quality & Testing Lab

800㎡ dedicated QC laboratory. Environmental chambers, X-Ray (2D + CT), microsection lab, impedance TDR testing, and custom functional test stations. ISO/IEC 17025 calibrated equipment with full traceability.

  • GE Phoenix X-Ray
  • Polar TDR
  • Keyence Microscope
  • Thermal Chamber
  • ICT/FCT Stations
  • Microsection Lab

Box-Build & Logistics Zone

2,500㎡ assembly and warehouse area for enclosure integration, wiring, conformal coating, and final packaging. Temperature-controlled component storage. Export packaging station with ESD-safe packing materials.

  • Assembly Workstations
  • Crimping Tools
  • UV Coating Booth
  • HI-POT Tester
  • Label Printer
  • Pallet Wrapper

Key Equipment Showcase
