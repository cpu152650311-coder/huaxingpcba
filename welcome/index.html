FAQ

Common Questions

What's your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
For PCB fabrication: MOQ is 1 piece for prototypes. For volume production, MOQ typically starts at 5–10 pieces. For SMT assembly: MOQ is 5 pieces. We're happy to handle small research batches alongside high-volume orders.
What are your typical lead times?
PCB prototypes: 24–72 hours. Standard PCB production: 5–7 working days. SMT assembly: 3–7 working days depending on complexity. Turnkey (PCB + assembly): 7–12 working days. Express options are available — ask your account manager.
Do you offer free design review (DFM)?
Yes. Every order includes a free DFM (Design for Manufacturability) review. Our engineers check your Gerber files for common issues — trace/space violations, annular ring problems, solder mask slivers, impedance mismatches — and send you a report within 4 hours. We never proceed to production until you approve.
What certifications do you hold?
We hold ISO 9001:2015 (quality management), IATF 16949:2016 (automotive quality), ISO 14001 (environmental), UL certification, RoHS & REACH compliance. Our assembly meets IPC-A-610 Class 3 standards. Certificates are available upon request — we're happy to share them during the qualification process.
How do you handle international shipping?
We ship to 30+ countries via DHL, FedEx, UPS, and TNT for express (3–5 days), or sea/air freight for volume orders. DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) terms are available — we handle customs clearance so you don't have to. All shipments are fully insured and tracked.
Is my design data protected?
Absolutely. We sign NDAs as standard practice. Your Gerber files, BOM, and design data are stored on access-controlled servers and never shared with third parties. We understand that PCB designs represent significant IP investment, and we treat them accordingly.
