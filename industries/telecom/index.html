When a Signal at 77 GHz Must Arrive Intact

At microwave frequencies, a PCB is no longer just a wiring platform — it becomes part of the circuit. Dielectric constant, loss tangent, copper surface roughness, and glass weave pattern all directly impact signal integrity. Huaxing's RF PCB line handles Rogers RO4000/RO3000, Taconic, Isola Astra, and Panasonic Megtron 6 laminates with TDR-verified controlled impedance at ±5% tolerance. Every RF board is tested with a vector network analyzer (VNA) before shipment.

RF/Microwave Materials

Not all PCB laminates are created equal. Standard FR-4 becomes lossy and unpredictable above ~2 GHz. We stock and process a full range of RF laminates, including hybrid constructions where RF-critical layers use expensive low-loss materials while power/digital layers use cost-optimized FR-4 — delivering performance where needed without blowing the BOM.

  • Rogers RO4350B, RO4003C, RO3003, RO3010
  • Panasonic Megtron 6 / Megtron 7 low-loss
  • Hybrid stackup: RF + FR-4 in same PCB
  • TDR impedance testing, VNA S-parameter validation

RF Laminate Portfolio

Dk 3.48

Rogers RO4350B

Hydrocarbon ceramic. Low loss, processable like FR-4. Ideal for 2-20 GHz power amplifiers, antennas, and couplers.

Dk 3.00

Rogers RO3003

PTFE-ceramic composite. Very low loss (tanδ 0.001). Stable Dk over temperature. For millimeter-wave and automotive radar.

Dk 3.60

Megtron 6

High-Tg low-loss FR-4 alternative. Excellent for high-speed digital (56Gbps+ PAM4). Cost-effective for mid-layer counts.

Telecom Applications

5G Base Stations

AAU (Active Antenna Unit) PCBs with 64T64R massive MIMO. Multi-layer RF + digital hybrid. Rogers + high-Tg FR-4 construction. Phase-stable through thermal cycles.

RF Power Amplifiers

GaN/SiGe PA PCBs on Rogers substrates. Thermal management with coin-attach technology. Wilkinson dividers and impedance-matching networks integrated in PCB.

Optical Networking

400G/800G optical transceiver PCBs. High-speed digital with Megtron 6. Flex-rigid for SFP/QSFP-DD form factors. ±5% impedance on 112Gbps PAM4 lanes.

Satellite Communication

LEO satellite bus and payload PCBs. PTFE laminates for space environment. Ultra-lightweight construction. Outgassing-compliant materials per NASA/TASA standards.

Data Center Interconnect

Backplane and line-card PCBs for 800G switches. 32+ layers with backdrill on every via. Ultra-low skew differential pairs. PAM4 signal integrity validated.

Antenna Systems

Phased array antenna PCBs. Beamforming feed networks with precise phase matching. Embedded antenna elements. Rogers and PTFE materials for consistent Dk.
