When a PCB Cannot Fail

A PCB inside a pacemaker, an infusion pump, or a CT scanner has zero margin for error. Our ISO 13485 medical manufacturing line operates under cleanroom conditions (Class 100K) with full UDI-compliant traceability from laminate lot to finished assembly. Every medical PCB ships with a Device History Record (DHR) and Certificate of Conformance. Since 2018, Huaxing has manufactured PCBs for FDA Class II and III medical devices with zero field failures.

ISO 13485 Quality System

Unlike ISO 9001 which focuses on customer satisfaction, ISO 13485 mandates risk management across the entire manufacturing chain. Every process change, material substitution, or equipment modification triggers a formal risk assessment. Our medical line maintains a validated state — all critical processes are IQ/OQ/PQ validated and re-validated annually.

  • ISO 13485:2016 certified medical manufacturing
  • Risk management per ISO 14971
  • IQ/OQ/PQ process validation
  • Full DHR (Device History Record) documentation

Cleanroom Assembly & Traceability

Huaxing's medical SMT line operates in a Class 100K (ISO 8) cleanroom with positive pressure, HEPA filtration, and continuous particle monitoring. Every component, from the smallest 0201 passive to complex BGA processors, is lot-tracked through the entire assembly process. Full forward and backward traceability enables rapid containment in the rare event of a supplier notification.

  • Class 100K (ISO 8) cleanroom SMT assembly
  • UDI-compliant full component traceability
  • Biocompatible solder and conformal coating options
  • ESD control per ANSI/ESD S20.20

Medical Applications

Implantable Devices

Ultra-miniaturized HDI PCBs for pacemakers, neurostimulators, and cochlear implants. Microvias down to 75µm laser drill. Biocompatible materials. IPC Class 3 with 100% inspection.

Diagnostic Imaging

High-layer-count backplanes for CT, MRI, and ultrasound systems. Controlled impedance ±5%, low signal loss materials (Rogers, Megtron 6). Large-format boards up to 600×800mm.

Wearable Monitors

Flex and rigid-flex PCBs for continuous glucose monitors, ECG patches, and pulse oximeters. Ultra-thin construction, dynamic flex zone design. Low-outgassing materials for skin-contact applications.

Surgical Instruments

High-reliability PCBs for electrosurgical units, surgical robots, and endoscopic systems. High-voltage isolation, sterilization-compatible materials. 100% HiPot + functional testing.

Patient Monitoring

Multi-parameter monitor PCBs with mixed-signal design. Precision analog front-end routing. EMI shielding for sensitive biopotential measurements. Extended lifecycle component management.

In-Vitro Diagnostics

Optical sensor PCBs for blood analyzers, PCR machines, and immunoassay systems. Gold-plated contacts for corrosion resistance. Precision alignment features. Cleanroom assembly.
