Why Automotive PCBs Are Fundamentally Different

A consumer electronics PCB operates at room temperature for 2-3 years. An automotive PCB must survive -40°C cold starts, 150°C under-hood temperatures, 85°C/85% RH humidity exposure, and continuous vibration — for 15 years. Every manufacturing decision cascades from this requirement. At Huaxing PCBA, our IATF 16949-certified facility has produced automotive-grade PCBs since 2015, with full PPAP documentation and zero field failures across 2 million+ shipped units.

IATF 16949 Compliance

IATF 16949 is not ISO 9001 with automotive branding. It mandates defect prevention over defect detection, full material lot traceability, and statistical process control (SPC) on every critical dimension. Huaxing's quality system is audited annually by TÜV — no non-conformances in the last 3 surveillance audits.

  • Full material traceability — laminate lot to finished PCB
  • PPAP Level 3 documentation package
  • SPC on all critical dimensions (etch, drill, impedance)
  • Annual IATF surveillance audit by TÜV — zero NCs

Thermal & Reliability Testing

Automotive PCBs face thermal cycles that would destroy consumer-grade boards. Huaxing performs 1,000-cycle thermal shock testing (-40°C to +125°C, 15-min dwell), CAF (Conductive Anodic Filament) resistance testing at 85°C/85% RH with 100V bias, and IST (Interconnect Stress Testing) per IPC-TM-650. Every automotive batch ships with a reliability coupon test report.

  • 1,000-cycle thermal shock (-40°C ⇄ +125°C)
  • CAF testing: 85°C/85% RH, 100V bias, 1,000 hours
  • IST coupon testing per IPC-TM-650
  • High-pot (HiPot) dielectric withstand testing

Automotive Applications

ADAS & Autonomous Driving

Radar, LiDAR, and camera sensor PCBs. Rogers/PTFE high-frequency laminates for 77GHz radar. Blind and buried vias for HDI sensor fusion boards. Controlled impedance ±5% on every signal layer.

Battery Management (BMS)

Heavy copper up to 12oz for high-current paths. High-Tg FR-4 and aluminum substrates for thermal dissipation. HiPot tested at 2,500V+. 100% flying probe + ICT on every board.

Engine Control Units (ECU)

High-layer-count (8-24 layers) with controlled impedance. Robust solder joints for under-hood vibration. Conformal coating against moisture and contaminants. IPC Class 3 acceptance throughout.

Infotainment & HMI

HDI any-layer for compact dash-mounted modules. Fine-pitch BGA assembly down to 0.3mm. EMI shielding with selective plating. High-reliability connectors for 100,000+ mating cycles.

LED Lighting Systems

Aluminum-core PCBs for direct thermal management. White solder mask for reflectivity. Large-format boards up to 600x800mm. Automotive-grade temperature cycling validated.

EV Powertrain

Inverter and DC-DC converter PCBs. 6-12oz copper for power stages. High-voltage isolation per IEC 60664. Thermal substrate technology for IGBT/SiC module mounting.
