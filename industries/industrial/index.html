Industrial PCBs Face the Harshest Conditions

A factory floor is unforgiving — vibration, dust, temperature swings, chemical exposure, and 24/7 operation. Consumer-grade PCBs fail within months under these conditions. At Huaxing PCBA, we manufacture industrial-grade PCBs designed for 10+ year service life, using high-Tg FR-4 (Tg170-Tg180), polyimide for extreme temperature applications, and heavy copper processing up to 12oz for high-current power stages. Every industrial board undergoes accelerated life testing to validate reliability before shipment.

Heavy Copper & High-Tg Technology

Motor drives and power converters need copper thicknesses that standard PCB shops won't touch. Huaxing's heavy copper line handles 4oz to 12oz copper with precision etching, maintaining 6/6mil trace/space even on 6oz copper layers. For mixed-technology boards (signal layers on thin copper, power layers on heavy copper), we use sequential lamination to bond dissimilar copper weights without delamination.

  • Heavy copper: 4oz / 6oz / 10oz / 12oz per layer
  • High-Tg FR-4: Tg170, Tg180 options
  • Polyimide for >180°C continuous operation
  • Sequential lamination for mixed copper weights

Key Capabilities

High-Voltage Isolation

Creepage and clearance per IEC 60664. HiPot testing at 2,500V+ with partial discharge monitoring. Slot and milling for isolation barriers.

Thermal Management

Aluminum and copper-core substrates. Thermal vias with 25µm copper plating. Bonded fin heat sinks integrated into PCB stackup.

Environmental Protection

Conformal coating (acrylic, silicone, urethane, parylene). IP-rated enclosures with gasketed connectors. 85°C/85% RH humidity testing.

Multi-Layer Backplanes

Up to 32 layers with blind/buried vias. Press-fit hole tolerances ±0.05mm. Controlled impedance on all signal layers.

Industrial Applications

PLC & Automation Controllers

High-reliability backplanes for modular PLC systems. DIN rail mounting compatibility. RJ45/USB/fieldbus connector arrays with reinforced solder joints.

Variable Frequency Drives

IGBT driver PCBs with 6-12oz copper. High-voltage isolation slots. Aluminum substrates for direct IGBT module mounting. 100% HiPot tested.

Industrial Power Supplies

Switch-mode PSU PCBs up to 10kW. Planar transformers integrated into PCB. Heavy copper for secondary side. Thermal management for passive cooling.

Motor Drives & Servos

Multi-axis servo drive PCBs. High-current traces with current sensing shunts. Encoder interface with controlled impedance. Conformal coating standard.

Industrial Sensors

Pressure, temperature, flow, and level sensor PCBs. Precision analog front-end routing. Intrinsically safe design per ATEX/IECEx. Small-form-factor HDI.

HMI & Operator Panels

Display driver PCBs with flex-to-board connections. Touchscreen controller integration. Wide-temperature LCD interface. IP65-compatible edge sealing.
