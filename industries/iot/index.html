IoT Demands What Conventional PCB Shops Can't Deliver

An IoT device PCB must be invisible to the end user — fitting inside a wall switch, a wristband, or a ceiling sensor. Yet it must also be a precision RF instrument, handling Bluetooth LE, Wi-Fi 6, Zigbee, Thread, LoRa, NB-IoT, or UWB — often multiple radios on the same board. Huaxing's IoT PCB capability combines any-layer HDI (down to 75µm laser microvias), rigid-flex for 3D packaging, and RF-optimized stackups with controlled impedance ±5%. We've shipped over 500,000 IoT device PCBs since 2020.

Any-Layer HDI & Miniaturization

Standard PCBs connect layers with through-hole vias that steal routing space on every layer they pass through. Any-layer HDI uses laser-drilled microvias connecting only the layers they need, freeing routing channels on adjacent layers. The result: an 8-layer HDI board can pack more routing density than a 14-layer conventional board — in half the thickness.

  • Any-layer HDI: 4-12 layers, laser µvias
  • 75µm laser drill, 0.3mm BGA pitch
  • 0201, 01005 component placement
  • Board thickness down to 0.3mm
