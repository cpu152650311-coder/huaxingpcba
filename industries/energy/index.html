Power Electronics: Where Every mΩ Matters

A solar inverter operates at 1,500V DC, switches at 20-100kHz, and must run for 25 years on a rooftop in direct sun. Every milli-ohm of trace resistance converts to heat that degrades components. At Huaxing PCBA, we manufacture power electronics PCBs with precision heavy-copper etching, thermal substrates, and 4kV isolation — engineered for efficiency and longevity in the most demanding energy applications.

Heavy Copper & High-Voltage Isolation

Power conversion demands copper thicknesses that handle hundreds of amps while maintaining precise geometry. Our heavy copper line processes 4oz to 12oz copper with controlled etching, producing clean trace profiles even on thick base copper. For high-voltage isolation, we design creepage slots, isolation barriers, and reinforced insulation per IEC 61800-5-1 and IEC 62109.

  • Heavy copper: 4-12oz, precision etching
  • 4kV+ HiPot testing with partial discharge monitoring
  • IEC 62109 (solar) & IEC 61800-5-1 (drives) isolation
  • Al/copper-core substrates for direct IGBT/SiC mounting

Power Electronics Capabilities

Planar Magnetics

PCB-integrated planar transformers and inductors. Optimized winding patterns for low leakage inductance. FR-4 and high-frequency laminate options.

Thermal Substrates

Aluminum-core (IMC) and copper-core PCBs. Direct-bonded copper (DBC) for SiC modules. Thermal vias arrays with filled-and-capped copper.

EMC Optimization

Power-stage layout for low EMI. Ground plane stitching for common-mode noise suppression. Shielded gate-drive routing for fast-switching SiC/GaN.

Bus Bar Integration

Laminated bus bar PCBs for low-inductance DC links. Press-fit and screw-terminal connections. Partial discharge-free design for >1kV DC links.

Energy Applications

Solar Inverters

String and central inverter PCBs up to 1,500V DC. 3-level NPC and T-type topologies. 25-year design lifetime. IEC 62109 safety isolation. Outdoor-rated with conformal coating.

EV Charging Stations

DC fast charger power-module PCBs (30-350kW). SiC MOSFET gate drive with isolation. CCS/NACS/CHAdeMO protocol boards. OCPP communication interface.

Battery Energy Storage

BMS master/slave PCBs for MWh-scale storage. Precision cell voltage sensing (mV accuracy). ISO-SPI isolated communication. Functional safety per IEC 61508 SIL-2.

Smart Grid & AMI

Smart meter and grid sensor PCBs. Precision metrology with <0.1% error. Power-line communication (G3-PLC). Tamper detection. 15+ year battery-powered operation.

Wind Power Converters

Generator-side and grid-side converter PCBs for multi-MW turbines. Heavy copper for rotor current paths. Low-Tg drift materials for nacelle temperature range.

UPS & Critical Power

Modular UPS power-module PCBs. Double-conversion topology with bidirectional DC-DC. Parallel operation with active load sharing. Hot-swap connector design.
