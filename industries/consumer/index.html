Consumer Applications

Smartphones & Tablets

Any-layer HDI mainboards with 8-12 layers. 0.3mm BGA pitch, 0201 passives. Fine-pitch FPC connectors. High-speed digital for LPDDR/USB-C/MIPI DSI.

Wearables & Earbuds

Ultra-compact rigid-flex PCBs for TWS earbuds, smartwatches, and fitness bands. 0.3mm total thickness. Curved flex for 3D packaging. Bluetooth antenna integration.

Audio Equipment

Premium audio PCBs with 2oz copper for low-impedance paths. Precision analog layout. Gold-plated contacts. Bluetooth/Wi-Fi combo for wireless speakers and soundbars.

Home Appliances

Smart home appliance control PCBs. WiFi + touch interface combos. AC-DC power supply integration. Appliance-grade reliability with 5+ year design life.

Gaming Peripherals

High-speed USB/Bluetooth gaming mouse and keyboard PCBs. Mechanical switch mounting with reinforced pads. RGB LED driver integration. Ultra-low latency design.

IoT Consumer Devices

Smart plugs, cameras, doorbells, and sensor PCBs. Wi-Fi/Thread/Zigbee compact modules. CE/FCC pre-certified designs. 100K+ volume capability.
